# sonia school of tech
sonia school of tech is a school that bridges the gap between theory learning and practical learning, ensuring kids have more time in practical than mastering theory. 

This school is the future

## Problem statement
In the industry of technology there is a gap between students who are very good at theory but in the field are stuck and the only exposure they get is in internships or work environment. 
**learning on the job**

## solution
sonia school of technology will solve this problem by sending students to the industry and exposing them to real world technology 80% of learning and theory is 20% of learning. ensuring students are good at what they do and still understand what they are doing
